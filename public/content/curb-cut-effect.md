# Curb Cut Effect

Good to know: Models of Disability

Check out next: Universal Design/Independent Living (IL)

## Explanation

- Curb Cut Effect is the phenomenon of how accessible design for disabled people unintentionally benefit the majority of society.

## Clarification

Under the medical/charity model:

<aside>
🏥

- Accessible design is viewed as a specialized modification that is a unqiue privilege for a small minority
</aside>

Under the social model: 

<aside>
📢

- Accessible design is viewed as a holistic benefit that is a collective responsibility to all of society
</aside>

By removing priorly masked difficulties we make navigating the environment less difficult for disabled people and more effortless for able-bodied people

## Application

<aside>
☝

**Action**

Building curb cuts at roadside ledges for wheelchair users

</aside>

<aside>
🤌

**Results**

Benefiting people with skateboards, luggage, trolleys, carriages etc.

</aside>
