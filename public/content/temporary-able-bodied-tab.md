# Temporary Able Bodied (TAB)

Good to know: Models of Disability/Impairment vs. Disability

Check out next: Universal Design/Independent Living (IL)

## Explanation

- Temporary Able Bodied states that disability is a flowing border that everyone would be incorporated in at a stage in their lives due to the eventual occurrence of impairments

## Clarification

Under the medical model:

<aside>
🪨

- Disability is seen as a static category that is completely separated from able bodied people
</aside>

Under the social model: 

<aside>
📊

- Disability is seen as a dynamic state that is abled bodied people are exempt from at present
- Currently able bodied people will eventually be exposed to obstacles currently masked by the absence of impairment
</aside>

TAB bridges the gap between disabled and able bodied people by portraying the dismantling of barriers as a necessary responsibility that all have a stake in instead of a charitable action benefiting a irrelevant minority

## Application

John cruise is currently able bodied, however he could be impaired through

1. Aging-Osteoarthritis due to accumulated wear on joints
2. Accidents-Unexpectedly losing a limb in a car accident
3. Illness-Developing autoimmune diseases that result in paralysis

Therefore he is not able bodied nor disabled, he is temporarily able bodied
